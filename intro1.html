Instagram Thanksgiving Site

This weekend we'll be working on a Thanksgiving-themed site that uses the Instagram API. There's no expectation that anyone here has done any web development work before--you can be a complete newbie.

Some things to keep in mind this weekend:

  • Don't get worried if it takes you awhile to figure something out, and don't be afraid to just start typing. Writing code isn't about "knowing the answer"--it's problem-solving, and you often have to just bang your head against it for awhile before it makes sense.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help. The whole point of this workshop is to work with beginners.

Our goals for this weekend:

  • Exposure to web development
  • Working examples to refer to later on your own
  • Experience Googling answers
  • Something cool to show off :)
